Michael Hall Owner
Michael Hall, originally from Pennsylvania, joined the Army to become a Generator Mechanic. He completed two combat tours in Iraq and Afghanistan and was stationed in Germany and Fort Campbell, KY. Over his five-year service, Michael also took on the role of manager of the Arms Room. After leaving the Army, Michael work for various diesel mechanic companies. with his vast knowledge, he spend many years running maintenance departments. In all work settings, Michael’s number one concern has been safe operations. Michael settled in Allen County, KY, with his wife Elisabeth Mlinko, daughter Alyssa Hall, and son Mason Mlinko.
